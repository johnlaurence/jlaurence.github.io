Chart Explanation
• The chart uses data from Electorencephalography (EEG) and Galvanic Skin Response (GSR) recordings made with a group of respondents while watching television commercials.
• The emotional valence (approach versus withdrawal) and emotional arousal (excited versus bored) are plotted on the x and y axis in real time.
• The chart uses sub-second resolution to accurately quantify each moment of a groups' response to the ad.
• The chart draws data from an online datasource and plots it in realtime against the video recording.
